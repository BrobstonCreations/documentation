# 2018-2020 Winnebago Revel Extra Large Kitchen Galley Rear Vent

### While this kit can be installed yourself, for best results we recommend having it professionally installed

### Tools Needed:
- Drill
- Drill bits
- Impact driver with philips bit or Philips screw driver
- Jigsaw
- Oscillating tool
- Adjustable Wrench
- Mechanical Pencil
- Straight edge (for drawing a line between pencil marks)

### Brackets and fasteners included in the kit:
- 1x - Extra Large Vent Bracket
- 16x - self tapping cap head screws

## Installation:

1. Remove the fridge door top fastener.
![3-remove-fridge-door-top-fastener.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/3-remove-fridge-door-top-fastener.jpg)

2. Tilt the fridge door forward and lift it off the bottom hinge.
![4-begin-removing-fridge-door.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/4-begin-removing-fridge-door.jpg)
![5-remove-fridge-door.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/5-remove-fridge-door.jpg)

3. Remove the four caps and screws from the front left and right sides of the fridge.
![6-remove-fridge-caps-and-screws.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/6-remove-fridge-caps-and-screws.jpg)

4. Begin removing the fridge.
![7-begin-removing-fridge.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/7-begin-removing-fridge.jpg)

5. Disconnect the fridge wiring.
![8-begin-disconnecting-fridge-wiring.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/8-begin-disconnecting-fridge-wiring.jpg)
![9-remove-fridge-wiring.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Kitchen-Galley-Microwave-Install-Instructions-images/9-remove-fridge-wiring.jpg)

6. Remove the induction cooktop. There are four screws between the original mounting bracket and the countertop and four screws between the mounting bracket and the cooktop.

7. Remove the sink drain. This should be possible by loosening the upper worm clamp on the top of the macerator through the opening inside the galley, to the right of fridge. You will likely also need to disconnect the p-trap from the rest of the drain to completely remove the drain tube.
8. Position the vent. The right edge of the vent should be just under 1/4 inch spaced away from the table. The top edge of the vent should be 3/16 of an inch below the bottom of the countertop.
9. While holding the vent in place, install the screws (feel free to drill a pilot hole, but it does not seem to be necessary in this case), I would recommend starting from the top and work your way down.
10. Mark the inside left and right of each vent opening with a mechanical pencil.
![1-PXL_20260227_161947553.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/1-PXL_20260227_161947553.jpg)

11. Remove the vent.
12. (This step isn't completely necessary, but later will help to show if your cut was square.) Use a straight edge to draw a line just outside of the pencil marks made in the last step.
13. Use a drill bit slightly smaller than the holes created from the screws that were previously installed to drill a few holes all the way through to the other side of the panel.
![3-PXL_20260227_170736187.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/3-PXL_20260227_170736187.jpg)

14. In the same orientation, but on the other size of the panel, screw the panel on from the inside.
15. Mark the inside left and right of each vent opening with a mechanical pencil.
16. Remove the vent.
17. Use a straight edge to draw a line just outside of the pencil marks made in the last step.
![4-PXL_20260227_183811368.MP.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/4-PXL_20260227_183811368.MP.jpg)

18. Use your drill and a drill bit bigger than the blade on your jigsaw to drill a hole inside of your cut area as a location to start your jigsaw cut.
19. Be sure that your jig saw blade is not too long that it will hit the door seal.
20. Carefully cut along the line from the inside of the kitchen galley, make the cut as straight as possible. You may need to cut the top and bottom with your oscillating tool.
![5-PXL_20260227_185341800.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/5-PXL_20260227_185341800.jpg)

21. Reinstall the vent from the outside of the kitchen galley.
![6-PXL_20260227_190022052.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/6-PXL_20260227_190022052.jpg)

22. Reinstall all parts that were removed in the reverse order.

23. You are finished!
