# 2018-2020 Winnebago Revel Extra Large Kitchen Galley Rear Vent

### Tools Needed:

### Brackets and fasteners included in the kit:

1. Remove the fridge
2. Position the vent. The right edge of the vent should be just under 1/4 inch spaced away from the table. The top edge of the vent should be 3/16 of an inch below the bottom of the countertop.
3. While holding the vent in place, install the screws (feel free to drill a pilot hole, but it does not seem to be necessary in this case), I would recommend starting from the top and work your way down.
4. Mark the inside left and right of each vent opening with a mechanical pencil.
![1-PXL_20260227_161947553.jpg](2018-2020-Winnebago-Revel-Extra-Large-Kitchen-Galley-Rear-Vent-images/1-PXL_20260227_161947553.jpg)
5. Remove the vent.
6. (This step isn't completely necessary, but later will help to show if your cut was square.) Use a straight edge to draw a line just outside of the pencil marks made in the last step.
7. Use a drill bit slightly smaller than the holes created from the screws that were previously installed to drill a few holes all the way through to the other side of the panel.
